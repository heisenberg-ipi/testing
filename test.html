Easy Chicken Biryani

Ingredients

For the rice:

  • 2 cups basmati rice, rinsed and soaked for 30 minutes
  • 4 cups water
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 4 cardamom pods
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1 tsp salt

For the chicken:

  • 500g chicken pieces, bone-in
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tbsp biryani masala (or garam masala)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • Fresh cilantro and mint leaves
  • 3 tbsp ghee or oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. In a pot, bring water to boil with bay leaves, cardamom, cinnamon and salt. Add rice and cook until 70% done (about 5-7 minutes). Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat ghee in a heavy-bottomed pot. Add sliced onions and fry until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies. Saut√© for 1 minute.
  4. Add chicken pieces and cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Add tomatoes, biryani masala, and salt. Cook until chicken is tender and oil separates (about 10 minutes).
  6. Layer half the partially cooked rice over the chicken. Sprinkle half the cilantro and mint leaves.
  7. Add remaining rice and top with the rest of the herbs.
  8. Cover with a tight lid and cook on low heat for 15-20 minutes.
  9. Gently mix the layers before serving.

Enjoy your flavorful homemade biryani!
