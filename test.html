Classic American Hamburger

Ingredients

For the patties:

  • 500g (1 lb) ground beef (80/20 lean-to-fat ratio)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce (optional)
  • 1 tsp garlic powder (optional)

For assembly:

  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 4 slices American or cheddar cheese
  • 4 lettuce leaves
  • 1 large tomato, sliced
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • Ketchup, mustard, and mayo for serving
  • Dill pickle slices

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, gently mix the ground beef with salt, pepper, and optional Worcestershire sauce and garlic powder. Don't overmix.
  2. Divide the meat into 4 equal portions and form into patties about 1/2 inch thick. Press a slight indent in the center of each with your thumb.
  3. Heat a grill or skillet to medium-high heat. For a skillet, add a small amount of oil.
  4. Cook patties for 3-4 minutes on each side for medium doneness.
  5. If using cheese, place a slice on each patty during the last minute of cooking.
  6. Toast the buns lightly on the grill or in a toaster.
  7. Spread desired condiments on the bottom bun, then add lettuce, patty, tomato, onion, and pickles.
  8. Cover with the top bun and serve immediately.

Enjoy your classic American hamburger!
