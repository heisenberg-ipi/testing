Classic Spaghetti with Tomato Sauce

Ingredients

For the sauce:

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tsp sugar (optional, to reduce acidity)

For the pasta:

  • 1 lb (450g) spaghetti
  • Salt for pasta water
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for serving
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish (optional)
  • Red pepper flakes (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and cook until soft, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add the garlic and cook for another 30 seconds until fragrant.
  3. Pour in the crushed tomatoes, dried herbs, salt, pepper, and sugar if using. Stir well.
  4. Bring to a simmer, then reduce heat to low and cook for 20-30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Meanwhile, bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  6. Add the spaghetti and cook according to package instructions until al dente, usually 8-10 minutes.
  7. Reserve 1/2 cup of pasta water, then drain the spaghetti.
  8. Add the drained pasta to the sauce, along with a splash of reserved pasta water if needed.
  9. Toss to coat the pasta evenly with the sauce.
  10. Serve immediately with grated Parmesan cheese, fresh basil, and red pepper flakes if desired.

Enjoy your classic spaghetti with tomato sauce!
