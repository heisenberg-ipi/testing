Basic Sushi Rolls

Ingredients

For the rice:

  • 2 cups Japanese short-grain rice
  • 2 cups water
  • ¼ cup rice vinegar
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt

For the rolls:

  • 4 sheets nori (seaweed)
  • 1 cucumber, cut into thin strips
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 200g sashimi-grade salmon or tuna (optional)
  • 4 crabsticks or cooked shrimp (optional)
  • Soy sauce for dipping
  • Wasabi and pickled ginger for serving

Instructions

  1. Rinse rice until water runs clear. Cook in a rice cooker or pot according to package instructions.
  2. Mix rice vinegar, sugar, and salt in a small bowl until dissolved to make sushi vinegar.
  3. Transfer cooked rice to a large wooden bowl. Sprinkle sushi vinegar over rice and fold gently to incorporate. Let cool to room temperature.
  4. Place a bamboo sushi mat on a clean surface. Put a sheet of nori on the mat, shiny side down.
  5. With wet hands, spread about ¾ cup of rice evenly over the nori, leaving 1 inch uncovered at the top.
  6. Arrange your fillings in a line across the center of the rice.
  7. Using the bamboo mat, roll the nori and rice over the fillings, applying gentle pressure.
  8. Continue rolling until you reach the uncovered edge of nori. Wet the edge slightly to seal.
  9. With a sharp, wet knife, cut each roll into 6-8 pieces.
  10. Serve with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger.

Enjoy your homemade sushi rolls!
