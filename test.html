Authentic Italian Pizza

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 500g (4 cups) "00" flour or all-purpose flour
  • 300ml (1¼ cups) lukewarm water
  • 7g (2 tsp) active dry yeast
  • 10g (2 tsp) salt
  • 15ml (1 tbsp) olive oil

For the topping:

  • 400g (14 oz) San Marzano tomatoes, crushed
  • 250g (8 oz) fresh mozzarella cheese, torn into pieces
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, dissolve the yeast in lukewarm water and let it sit for 5-10 minutes until foamy.
  2. Add half of the flour to the bowl and mix until combined. Add the salt and olive oil, then gradually add the remaining flour until a soft dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough for about 10 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  4. Place the dough in an oiled bowl, cover, and let it rise for 2 hours or until doubled in size.
  5. Preheat your oven to the highest temperature (usually 475-500°F/250-260°C).
  6. Divide the dough into 2 equal pieces. Form each piece into a ball and let rest for 30 minutes.
  7. Stretch or roll one dough ball into a 12-inch circle.
  8. Spread crushed tomatoes over the dough, leaving a small border around the edge.
  9. Add mozzarella pieces evenly across the pizza.
  10. Bake for 8-10 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  11. Top with olive oil and fresh basil leaves.

Enjoy your authentic Italian pizza!
